    • About Animals

    Animals are living creatures that play a vital role in our world.They come in many different types, such as mammals, birds, reptiles, and more.Animals provide food, help in farming, and keep our environment balanced.Some animals are common, while others are very rare and need special care to survive.


    Animals Rule In The Ecosystem

    Animals are a key part of the ecosystem.Each species has a role, like bees that help pollinate plants, or wolves that keep other animal populations under control.When one species disappears, it can harm the whole balance of nature.This is why protecting endangered animals is very important for our planet.
